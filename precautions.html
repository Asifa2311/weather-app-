Weather Precaution Tips

Summer Precautions

  • Stay hydrated
  • Avoid peak hours
  • Wear light clothing
  • Apply sunscreen

Rainy Season Precautions

  • Keep rain protection
  • Avoid stagnant water
  • Wear waterproof shoes
  • Keep surroundings dry

Winter Precautions

  • Wear warm clothes
  • Limit outdoor activities
  • Ventilate rooms
  • Use moisturizer
